How to Install & Play the Dodge Game

Step 1: Open the Game Link

Use your browser (preferably Chrome or Edge) and open the game link:

https://idowu123.github.io/dodgegame/

Step 2: Add the Game to Your Home Screen

  1. Wait for the game to fully load on your phone.
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner of your browser.
  3. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install App".
  4. Confirm by tapping Add.
  5. The game will now appear as an icon on your phone — just like a real app!

Step 3: How to Play Online and Offline

When you first open the game, make sure your data connection is ON so that it can install fully.

After that, you can turn off your data connection — the game will still work offline without internet!

Step 4: Share the Game with Friends

You can support the developer by sharing the game with your friends:

  • Copy and send the game link via WhatsApp, Facebook, or Telegram.
  • You can also send the game folder via Bluetooth or file-sharing apps.
  • Encourage your friends to add it to their home screen and enjoy offline play too!
